## Implemented architecture

- Three independent versioned persistence domains and key families.
- Safe injected browser-storage and event adapters.
- Per-module learner-artifact and Reflection stores with independent hooks, events, clear operations, and failure states.
- Written-response and branching-simulation discriminated models.
- Opaque current-revision identities and exact written, Reflection, and branching review-subject checks.
- Latest-only replacement, stale association rejection, pending-state recovery, malformed sibling isolation, unsupported-version blocking, raw unreadable-record preservation, and baseline-aware session retry.
- Legacy `course-progress` migration with authoritative-slug filtering, current envelope, reset, and same/cross-tab subscriptions.
- Accessible progress-reset disclosure and confirmation on the dashboard, with focus moved to the result after confirmation.
- Bounded session-only/save-failed completion notices on the amendment-authorized module and Orientation surfaces.
- Vitest/jsdom architecture tests expanded from 17 to 34 risk-matrix cases, plus supplemental coverage reporting.

No Reverse Build v2 rendering, Module 4 content/logic/UI/API, Design Practice or Reflection UI integration, AI route/provider/prompt change, account, authentication, database, cloud persistence, cross-device synchronization, history, analytics, scoring, diagnosis, or personalization was implemented.
